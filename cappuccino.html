Cappuccino Coffee Selection

Traditional Italian Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Balanced, rich espresso flavor with creamy, velvety milk and light sweetness. Slight bitterness from the espresso is softened by the foam.

Recommended Brew: Brew 1 shot (30 ml) of espresso.
Steam 100 ml of milk until it reaches 65–70°C (149–158°F), creating microfoam.
Pour steamed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam slightly.
Spoon 1–2 cm of foam on top.
Ratio: 1/3 espresso, 1/3 steamed milk, 1/3 foam.

Dry Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Stronger coffee flavor, lighter body. Less creamy, more intense espresso profile due to less milk.

Recommended Brew: Brew 1 shot of espresso.
Steam a small amount of milk, creating mostly foam.
Add just a small splash of steamed milk, then top with a thick layer of foam (about 2/3 of the cup).
Difference: More foam, less milk—ideal for bold espresso lovers.

$3.9

Wet Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Smooth, creamier, slightly sweeter. Milder coffee presence.

Recommended Brew: Brew 1 shot of espresso.
Steam more milk than foam, similar to a latte.
Pour the steamed milk directly over the espresso and top with a little foam.
Ratio: Closer to 1/2 milk, 1/4 espresso, 1/4 foam.

$4.5

Iced Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Chilled, refreshing, lighter body. Espresso notes still prominent, less creamy.

Recommended Brew: Brew a double shot of espresso and let it cool.
Froth cold milk using a milk frother or French press.
Pour espresso over ice, then add frothed cold milk and foam.
Optional: Add a dash of vanilla syrup or cinnamon.

$4.9

Flavored Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Sweet and aromatic with a distinct flavored twist. Espresso may take a backseat to syrup flavor.

Recommended Brew: Brew 1 shot of espresso.
Add 1–2 pumps of flavored syrup (vanilla, caramel, mocha).
Steam milk and pour into the cup, then top with foam.
Garnish (optional): drizzle of sauce, cocoa powder, or cinnamon.
Tip: Use sugar-free syrup for a lighter version.

$4.8

Vegan or Non-Dairy Cappuccino

Tasting Notes: Nutty or creamy depending on the milk (e.g. almond, oat, soy). Can be slightly sweeter or earthier.

Recommended Brew: Brew 1 shot of espresso.
Steam your preferred non-dairy milk (oat works best for good foam).
Pour steamed milk and foam over espresso.
Optional: Sprinkle cinnamon or cocoa powder.
Note: Use barista-blend plant milks for better frothing results.

$5
