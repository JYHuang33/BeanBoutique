Latte Coffee Selection

Classic Caffè Latte

Tasting Notes: Smooth, mild, and creamy with a gentle espresso flavor. The milk balances bitterness, making it sweet and mellow.

Recommended Brew:Brew 1–2 shots of espresso (30–60 ml).
Steam 200 ml of milk until it’s velvety and reaches around 65°C (149°F).
Pour milk slowly into the espresso, holding back foam.
Top with a thin layer of foam (≈1 cm).

Price : $3.5

Vanilla Latte

Tasting Notes:Creamy and sweet with warm vanilla notes that soften the espresso. Comforting and easy-drinking.

Recommended Brew:Add 1–2 pumps of vanilla syrup to your cup.
Brew 1–2 espresso shots.
Steam 200 ml milk and pour over the espresso.
Stir gently and optionally top with vanilla powder or whipped cream.

Price : $4

Iced Latte

Tasting Notes:Light, chilled, and refreshing. Smooth milk flavor dominates with a clean coffee undertone.

Recommended Brew: Brew a double espresso and let it cool.
Fill a tall glass with ice.
Add espresso over ice.
Pour in cold milk (150–200 ml), stir, and serve with a straw.

Price : $4.5

Caramel Latte

Tasting Notes:Sweet and buttery caramel blends beautifully with the espresso’s roastiness. Rich, indulgent, and dessert-like.

Recommended Brew:Brew 1–2 shots of espresso.
Add 1–2 pumps of caramel syrup.
Steam 200 ml milk and pour over.
Drizzle caramel sauce on top.

Price : $4.9

Matcha Latte

Tasting Notes: Earthy and grassy from the matcha, with a creamy sweetness from the milk. Caffeine is gentler than coffee.

Recommended Brew:Whisk 1–2 tsp of matcha powder with hot water (60–70°C).
Steam or froth 200 ml of milk.
Pour milk into matcha base.
Stir gently, and top with matcha dust if desired.

Price : $3.9

Chai Latte

Tasting Notes: Spiced and aromatic. Notes of cinnamon, cardamom, and clove create a warm, cozy experience.

Recommended Brew:Steep chai tea bag or chai concentrate in hot water.
Steam 200 ml milk and add to the chai.
Stir in honey or brown sugar if desired.
Top with foam and sprinkle cinnamon or nutmeg.

Price : $4.3
